## REA-02

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Completeness deviation
  - Article: Art. 15
  - GDPR Requirement: Data subjects have the right to access their personal data and information about its processing.
  - Policy Realization: The policy limits the right of access if confidential information would be disclosed, without mentioning GDPR conditions for such limitations.

## REA-04

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Completeness deviation
  - Article: Art. 17
  - GDPR Requirement: Data subjects have the right to erasure of personal data under certain conditions, with specific exceptions.
  - Policy Realization: The policy omits exceptions such as public interest or scientific research from the right to erasure.

## REA-05

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Completeness deviation
  - Article: Art. 18
  - GDPR Requirement: Data subjects have the right to restrict processing under specific conditions.
  - Policy Realization: The policy does not specify the conditions under which processing can be restricted.

## REA-07

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Completeness deviation
  - Article: Art. 21
  - GDPR Requirement: Data subjects have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests and for direct marketing purposes.
