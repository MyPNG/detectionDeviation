# LLM Deviation Review

## REG-032
- Clause: Art17(1)
- Considered References: Art6(1), Art8(2), Art21(5), Art21(6)

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: the data subject withdraws consent on which the processing is based according to point (a) of Article 6(1), or point (a) of Article 9(2), and where there is no other legal ground for the processing
  - Policy Quote: It is removed upon your withdrawal of consent or your request to terminate these services.
  - Mismatch: The policy commits to erase data upon consent withdrawal regardless of whether another legal ground exists, while GDPR requires erasure only if no other legal ground remains.
  - Analysis: GDPR allows continued processing on another lawful basis after consent withdrawal; the policy’s unconditional erasure is stricter than required.

## REG-021
- Clause: Art13(3)
- Considered References: Art14(5)

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: Where the controller intends to further process the personal data for a purpose other than that for which the personal data were collected, the controller shall provide the data subject prior to that further processing with information on that other purpose and with any relevant further information as referred to in paragraph 2.
  - Policy Quote: Your data is not used for any other purposes or shared with third parties.
  - Mismatch: GDPR permits further processing with prior information, whereas the policy categorically forbids any other purposes.
  - Analysis: By banning further purposes entirely, the policy imposes a stricter constraint than GDPR, which would allow such processing with advance notice including Art 13(2) details.

## REG-026
- Clause: Art14(4)
- Considered References: Art15(4)

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: Where the controller intends to further process the personal data for a purpose other than that for which the personal data were obtained, the controller shall provide the data subject prior to that further processing with information on that other purpose and with any relevant further information as referred to in paragraph 2.
  - Policy Quote: Your data is not used for any other purposes or shared with third parties.
  - Mismatch: GDPR allows further processing with prior information (including Art 14(2) items), while the policy categorically prohibits any other purposes.
  - Analysis: The policy’s explicit prohibition on further purposes is stricter than GDPR’s conditional allowance to further process with prior disclosure; this is an over-compliance.
