# LLM Deviation Review

## REG-006
- Clause: Art9(4)
- Considered References: Art6(4)

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: The risk management measures referred to in paragraph 2, point (d), shall give due consideration to the effects and possible interaction resulting from the combined application of the requirements set out in this Section, with a view to minimising risks more effectively while achieving an appropriate balance in implementing the measures to fulfil those requirements. ... The risk management system shall be understood as a continuous iterative process planned and run throughout the entire lifecycle of a high-risk AI system
  - Policy Quote: Applies to: All AI systems.
  - Mismatch: Article 9(4), via Art 9(2)(d), concerns measures for high-risk AI systems, while the policy applies the assessment requirement to all AI systems, which is stricter than required.
  - Analysis: Paragraph 4’s obligations attach to the risk management system under 9(2)(d) for high-risk AI systems; extending equivalent assessment obligations to all AI systems expands the regulatory scope and constitutes over-compliance.

## REG-004
- Clause: Art9(2)
- Considered References: None

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: The risk management system shall be understood as a continuous iterative process planned and run throughout the entire lifecycle of a high-risk AI system
  - Policy Quote: Applies to: All AI systems.
  - Mismatch: Article 9(2) limits the obligation to high-risk AI systems, whereas the policy requires assessments for all AI systems, which is stricter than required.
  - Analysis: The regulation’s obligations apply to high-risk AI systems only; the policy’s blanket requirement for all AI systems represents an expansion in scope (over-compliance) without contradicting the regulation.
