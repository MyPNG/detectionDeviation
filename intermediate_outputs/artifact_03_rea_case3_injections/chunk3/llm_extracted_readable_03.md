# LLM Deviation Review

## REG-013
- Clause: Art10(1)
- Considered References: Art13(3)

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: High-risk AI systems which make use of techniques involving the training of AI models with data shall be developed on the basis of training, validation and testing data sets that meet the quality criteria referred to in paragraphs 2 to 5 whenever such data sets are used.
  - Policy Quote: Applies to: All AI systems.
  - Mismatch: The policy expands applicability from only high-risk AI systems to all AI systems.
  - Analysis: Art10(1) applies specifically to high-risk AI systems; the policy imposes its requirement set on all AI systems, which is stricter than required. Art10(2) (REG-014) was reviewed; omissions about its details in the policy are treated as Non-Deviation.

## REG-018
- Clause: Art10(6)
- Considered References: Art13(3)

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: For the development of high-risk AI systems not using techniques involving the training of AI models, paragraphs 2 to 5 apply only to the testing data sets.
  - Policy Quote: Applies to: All AI systems.
  - Mismatch: The policy expands applicability from only high-risk AI systems to all AI systems.
  - Analysis: Art10(6) limits obligations to high-risk AI systems and, in that case, only to testing datasets; the policy’s blanket scope covers all AI systems, which is stricter. Art10(2) (REG-014) was considered; lack of explicit treatment in the policy is an omission (Non-Deviation).

## REG-046
- Clause: Art26(4)
- Considered References: Art22(3), Art22(4)

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: Without prejudice to paragraphs 1 and 2, to the extent the deployer exercises control over the input data, that deployer shall ensure that input data is relevant and sufficiently representative in view of the intended purpose of the high-risk AI system.
  - Policy Quote: Applies to: All AI systems.
  - Mismatch: The policy expands applicability from only high-risk AI systems to all AI systems.
  - Analysis: Art26(4) applies to high-risk AI systems and places an obligation on the deployer; the policy applies its requirements to all AI systems, which is stricter. Art26(1) (REG-043) and Art26(2) (REG-044) were considered; the policy’s lack of explicit deployer assignment is an omission, not a contradiction.
