# LLM Deviation Review

## REG-035
- Clause: Art14(3)
- Considered References: Art20(2), Art23(1)

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: The oversight measures shall be commensurate with the risks, level of autonomy and context of use of the high-risk AI system...
  - Policy Quote: Applies to: All AI systems.
  - Mismatch: The regulation targets oversight for high-risk AI systems, while the policy scopes oversight processes to all AI systems (broader/stricter scope).
  - Analysis: Policy extends oversight applicability beyond high-risk AI systems; this is over-compliance in scope, not a contradiction.
