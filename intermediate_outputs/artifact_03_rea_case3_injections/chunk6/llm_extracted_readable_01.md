## REG-027
- Clause: Art13(3)
- Considered References: None

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Negation deviation
  - GDPR Quote: The instructions for use shall contain at least the following information: (a) the identity and the contact details of the provider and, where applicable, of its authorised representative; (b) the characteristics, capabilities and limitations of performance of the high-risk AI system, including: (i) its intended purpose;
  - Policy Quote: 1) remove the intended use from customer-facing materials and make current customers aware of the issue, take action to close the identified gap, or discontinue the system,
  - Mismatch: While the regulation requires that instructions for use include the intended purpose, the policy directs removing the intended use from customer-facing materials when evidence is lacking.
  - Analysis: Customer-facing materials typically include instructions for use; authorizing removal of the intended use results in documentation that omits the required intended purpose, contradicting the mandate that it shall be included.
