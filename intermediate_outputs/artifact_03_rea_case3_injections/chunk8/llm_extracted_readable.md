## REG-036
- Clause: Art14(4)
- Considered References: Art20(3), Art20(4), Art21(1)

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: For the purpose of implementing paragraphs 1, 2 and 3, the high-risk AI system shall be provided to the deployer in such a way that natural persons to whom human oversight is assigned are enabled, as appropriate and proportionate: ...
  - Policy Quote: Applies to: All AI systems.
  - Mismatch: The policy applies the oversight-enablement requirements to all AI systems, whereas the regulation limits them to high-risk AI systems.
  - Analysis: Article 14(4) expressly addresses enabling oversight for high-risk AI systems; the policy expands this requirement to all AI systems, which is stricter than required.

## REG-033
- Clause: Art14(1)
- Considered References: None

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: High-risk AI systems shall be designed and developed in such a way, including with appropriate human-machine interface tools, that they can be effectively overseen by natural persons during the period in which they are in use.
  - Policy Quote: Applies to: All AI systems.
  - Mismatch: The policy extends the human-oversight design requirement beyond high-risk AI systems to all AI systems.
  - Analysis: The regulation confines this oversight-by-design obligation to high-risk AI systems; the policy applies it more broadly, which is over-compliant.

## REG-034
- Clause: Art14(2)
- Considered References: None

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: Human oversight shall aim to prevent or minimise the risks to health, safety or fundamental rights that may emerge when a high-risk AI system is used...
  - Policy Quote: Applies to: All AI systems.
  - Mismatch: The policy generalizes human-oversight risk mitigation obligations to all AI systems, while the regulation targets high-risk AI systems.
  - Analysis: Article 14(2) frames oversight objectives specifically for high-risk AI systems; the policy’s scope is broader and thus stricter.
