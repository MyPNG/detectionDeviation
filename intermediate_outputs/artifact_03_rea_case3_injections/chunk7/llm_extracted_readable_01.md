# LLM Deviation Review

## REG-014
- Clause: Art10(2)
- Considered References: None

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: Training, validation and testing data sets shall be subject to data governance and management practices appropriate for the intended purpose of the high-risk AI system.
  - Policy Quote: Applies to: All AI systems.
  - Mismatch: The regulation targets high-risk AI systems, while the policy applies these practices to all AI systems.
  - Analysis: The policy broadens the scope beyond the regulation’s high-risk AI system limitation, constituting over-compliance in scope.

## REG-015
- Clause: Art10(3)
- Considered References: None

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: including, where applicable, as regards the persons or groups of persons in relation to whom the high-risk AI system is intended to be used.
  - Policy Quote: Applies to: All AI systems.
  - Mismatch: The regulation’s dataset quality/statistical property requirements are framed in relation to high-risk AI systems, while the policy applies them to all AI systems.
  - Analysis: By extending requirements to all AI systems, the policy is stricter in scope than the regulation specifies.

## REG-016

- Considered References: None

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1

  - GDPR Quote: within which the high-risk AI system is intended to be used.

  - Mismatch: The regulation’s requirement to account for contextual characteristics applies to high-risk AI systems, while the policy applies it to all AI systems.
