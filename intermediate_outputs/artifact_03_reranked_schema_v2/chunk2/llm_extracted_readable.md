# LLM Deviation Review

## REG-092
- Clause: Art77(1)
- Considered References: None

- Deviation Count: 1

- Deviation 1
  - Type: Severity deviation
  - GDPR Quote: if the data subject considers that the processing of personal data relating to him or her infringes this Regulation.
  - Policy Quote: if you believe that the processing of your personal data infringes data protection law.
  - Mismatch: The policy broadens the condition for lodging a complaint from infringements of “this Regulation” (GDPR) to infringements of generic “data protection law.”
  - Analysis: GDPR Art 77(1) ties the complaint right to infringements of the GDPR specifically, whereas the policy extends it to any data protection law, making it more permissive than required.
